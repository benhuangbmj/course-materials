## Statistics on the Algebra I Regents Exam

Statistics accounts for **5â€“10%** of the Algebra I Regents Exam. It is relatively independent of other algebra skills and relies more on **quantitative literacy**. In addition, almost all required calculations can be completed using a **TI-84 calculator**.  
For these reasons, statistics problems are **must-do questions** and often the **best entry point** for building momentum on the exam.
