\noindent
{\bf Problem.}
Suppose $\phi$ is a function with a continuous derivative on $0 \leq x \leq 1$ satisfying there $\phi'(x) -2\phi(x) \leq 1$, and $\phi(0) = 1$. Prove that
\[
  \phi(x) \leq \frac{3}{2}e^{2x} - \frac{1}{2}.
\]
