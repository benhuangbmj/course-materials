\begin{frame}
\frametitle{The Hierarchy of Matrices}
Prominent properties of diagonal matrices:\pause
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
1&0&0\\
0&2&0\\
0&0&3
\end{bmatrix}^{-1} = \begin{bmatrix}
1&0&0\\
0&1/2&0\\
0&0&1/3
\end{bmatrix}
\]\pause
More generally, 
\[
\begin{bmatrix}
1&0&0\\
0&2&0\\
0&0&3
\end{bmatrix}^k = \begin{bmatrix}
1^k&0&0\\
0&2^k&0\\
0&0&3^k
\end{bmatrix}
\]
\end{frame}
