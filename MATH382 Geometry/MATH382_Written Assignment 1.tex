\noindent {\bf Problem.}
Let $\Phi$ be the Minkowski inner product, \(\mathbf r(x_1,x_2) = \left(x_1,x_2, \sqrt{1+x_1^2+x_2^2}\right)\), and $g_{ij} = \Phi(\mathbf r_{x_i}, \mathbf r_{x_j})$. Show that 
\[
\det{\left(
\begin{bmatrix}
g_{ij}
\end{bmatrix}\right)} = \frac{1}{{1+x_1^2+x_2^2}}
\]
