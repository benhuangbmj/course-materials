Let \(f(x) = x^3\). 
\begin{enumerate}
\item Pick a random point $A$ on the graph of $f(x)$. 
{\bf Point $A$}: \rule{10em}{0.1pt}
\item Let $B$ be an arbitrary point on the graph of $f(x)$ distinct from $A$. Write the slope of the line $AB$ as a function $g(x)$ of the $x$ coordinate of Point $B$
\vspace{\stretch{1}}
\item 
Find the limit of $g(x)$ as $B$ approaches $A$.
\vspace{\stretch{1}}
\clearpage
\item Find an equation of the tangent line of $f(x)$ at point $A$.
\vspace{\stretch{1}}
\item Plot $f(x)$, Point $A$, and the tangent line in a coordinate system.
\vspace{\stretch{1}}
\end{enumerate}
